About Personal Loan

A Personal Loan is a form of unsecured loan availed by individuals on the basis of specific criteria, such as level of income, employment history, credit score, and scope for repayment. The loan is usually taken for the fulfillment of personal needs from a banking institution or a non-banking financial company (NBFC). A Personal loan is taken by an Individual to fulfill his/her personal needs. Repayment can be done through fixed EMI over a fixed term. No guarantor or co-applicant is required.

Personal Loan comprises of the following

  • Purchase of a Flat (under construction or ready)/Bungalow/etc
  • Business needs
  • Marriage
  • Debt consolidations
  • Purchasing Assets
  • Other personal requirements
