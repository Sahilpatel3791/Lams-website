Borrower

What are the types of loans that Borrower can avail through VfinanceS support?

Borrower can avail various types of loans through VfinanceS support that includes Home Loans, Loan Against Properties, Secured Business Loans, Working Capital Loans, Education Loans, Car Loans, Overdraft Facilities, Dropline Overdraft Facilities, Bank Guarantee, CC Facilities, Term Loans, Loans Against FDs, Loan Against Securities, Project Finance, Private Equity Finance, Gold Loans, Other loan types. We are in the process to continuous improve our platform to enable easy access to all financial requirements for the borrowers.

Why should VfinanceS be used to apply for loans?

There are multiple reasons to join VfinanceS including – 1 Form – multiple lenders – multiple loans, Just 1 click to approach multiple lenders to avail funds, Easy decision making as now you don’t need to run behind lenders but lenders approach you directly through our platform, Transparent process, Completely paperless Application, Completely free platform to use, Time saving, etc.

Who will be the lenders for the Loans?

We are currently partnered with various Banks, NBFCs and Financial Institutions. However we are in the process to continuously keep including various Lenders on our platform.
