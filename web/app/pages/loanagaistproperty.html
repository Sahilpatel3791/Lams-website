About Loan Against Property

A Loan Against Property is an amount borrowed by individuals for a fixed tenure from financial institutions to either use the funds for some personal, business or any other reason as deem fit by the borrower subject to the same being approved by the lenders.

Loan Against Property is basically Loan taken by mortgaging a residential or commercial property for acquiring funds against it.

Loan Against Property comprises of the following

  • Funds requirements for business expansions or business usage
  • Personal requirements
  • Top Up Loans
  • Balance transfer of Loans Against Property
  • Balance transfer of Home Loans
